Let's Calculate Your Fitness Parameters

Enter your physiological parameters in the form and click Analyze Health Metrics. We will check the following indicators:

  • Body Mass Index (BMI): Classifies weight status and highlights clinical weight risks.
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR): Your body's baseline energy expenditure at complete rest.
  • Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE): Maintenance calories incorporating daily physical movement.
  • Body Fat Percentage: Checks ratio of adipose tissue using anthropometric calculations.
  • Ideal Body Weight Range: Benchmarks your metrics against healthy physiological norms.
